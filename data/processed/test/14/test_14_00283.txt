USE OF BABY HAMSTER LUNG CELL CULTURES

ESTABLISHMENT OF PRIMARY BABY HAMSTER LUNG CULTURES

After the seasonal decrease in breeding, baby hamsters are now again

reference cigarette smoke and preliminary resulte will be given in
our next report. .
