Analysis of Secretion from Dogs Used in The NCI Smoking
Experiment a

Analyses are in progress comparing protein levels in
serum and lung secretions from dogs smoking different
types of cigarettes. Both serum and bronchial mucus has
been collected from dogs exposed to high, medium and low
tar cigarettes and an air-exposed group. Dogs exposed
to PMO smoke had been sacrificed prior to the request
for samples and, unfortunately, these samples were not
available.

statistical analysis will be presented for each group. A
short description of the technical procedure and a typical
scanner chart is presented in Appendix I and II.
