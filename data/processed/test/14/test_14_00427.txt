Tobacco Extrusion

Objective:

Develop an extruded foam tobacco product.
Status:
Product

Samples prepared wherein chitin and chitosan were substituted for
starch in the formulation were found to improve rod firmness during
stoking and subjective response. Additional samples were produced
with chitan and chitosan replacing CHC (carboxymethylcellulose} in
the formulation. These samples will be evaluated by the Flayor Group.

Extruded rads containing 0.22, 0.4%, 2%, and 4% Magnesium Sulfate
were produced for evaluation {n Project Hamlet. Samples with 5%
Magnesium Oxide were also prepared.
