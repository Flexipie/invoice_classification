Cooked Flavor (2307)
A, Asparagine Determination (2)

Analytical Method Number E-50 was successfully adapted for
AutoAnalyzer 11 determination of asparagine in cooked flavors.
This procedure will replace the HPLC procedure for asparagine. A
special report has been written and will be issued shortly.

B. Amino Sugars (2)
Workhas begun on development of an amino sugar determination

in cooked flavors. The AutoAnalyzer methodology developed by Davis
for amino sugars in tobacco leaf and filler will be utilized (3).

C, Aldehydes (2)

A cursory examination of several cocked flavor samptes has
shown little difference in the aldehyde content. An AutoAnalyzer
procedure was used but the results were not quantitated. The
procedure will be worked out if differences are indicated.
