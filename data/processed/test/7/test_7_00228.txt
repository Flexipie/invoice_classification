RE: PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT ~ MAGNA/KN

MAGNA

Given MAGNA's recent product performance, the Brand anticipates
product development activity in both 1987 and 1988. The Brand's
two largest styles, FF 85 (SP) and FFLT are now inferior to
Marlboro among the target. This drop in performance is due
primarily to an increase in Marlboro's rating. However, MAGNA
has the following attribute deficiencies:

FF 85 (SP)
* less smooth
less mild
less fresh aftertaste

FPLT
less tobacco taste
not enough strength
less fresh aftertaste
less rich taste
harder draw

It is critical that MAGNA maintain a parity rating versus
Marlboro on all three styles, especially given the recent
repositioning of MAGNA as a full price brand.

KN

Objective

Identify menthol products which best meet the
performance/taste wants of younger adult target smokers.

Strategy

Establish a benchmark ideal against which to develop EN
product.
