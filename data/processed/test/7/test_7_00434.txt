OBJECTIVE:

To determine the degree of agreement between the analytical
labs currently being used for project ALPHA.

SUMMARY:

The 3 labs used for analyais of ALPHA product, each smoked 8
ports, 5 cigarettes per port by FTC and 50/30 smoking
conditions. Results for: WIPM, nicotine, B3, water, and CO
were compared for variability in the methods, and for the
agreement in mean values.

CONCLUSIONS:

Significant differences in measurement variability were found
for FTC: nicotine, and water analyses, Significant
differences were found in the mean analytical values among
the labs for both FTC and 60/30 smoking conditions on: WTPM,
nicotine, B3, and CO, and for FTC water analysis,

foung above, making any corrections, and repeating this
