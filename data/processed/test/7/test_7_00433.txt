Dear Dan:

tn case you haven't seen it, I am sending you the Congressional
Record pages on which appears Senator Kennedy's ringing
endorsement of his new bill--S. 968, the *“Bealth Sciences
Promotion Act of 1979."

You will find it not without certain ironies.

First, his series of rhetorical questions (page $4471) are
not. so rhetorical. Nor is his rhetorical answer—-“health
science research." Second, his broadcast blame of “Congress”
for the disease-a-month syndrome is a condition which some
observers believe he helped cause. ( #4772)

More intriguing, however, is the thicket of legalese in
sections 202 and 203 (pages $4475-76}. What does it mean?
The return of the NCI comet to its old orbit in the NIH
satellite system?

This letter and attachment are for your use as you see
fit but not for attribution.

est regards,

Fred
