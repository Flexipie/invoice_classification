BRANDS DEVELOPMENT

WINSTON KS/CANEL Filter 85

Full Flavor 85 PGT results were received this week for a ‘tar‘/draft
optimization study. High and low ‘tar’ levels (18-14 mg) were combined
with three draft levels (low-mediun-high) to identify configuration
preferences of full flavor smokers. Medium draft combined with lower
*tar’ (14.5 ng) consistently showed improvements in smoothness,
harshness, mildness, and mean thermometer over any other draft/’ tar’
combination.

A PGT was mailed last week to Full Flavor Smokers to examine moisture
(2 levels) and density (4 levels) variables and their effect on
consumer perceptions. The base product in this test was developed
utilizing learning gained from the Hunan Smoking Behavior study. These
results are due 3/82 and will be combined with the above configuration
study for future product development.
