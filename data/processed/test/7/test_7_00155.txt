Only some of our tipping specification ranges exceed 425% of target.
It is only these that I propose that we change. Our current tipping
specification ranges are given on the attached sheet.

We do not now have a guide for setting tipping specifications. As
our suppliers have indicated that they can supply tipping at 125% of
target with no upcharge, We should certainly not have specifications that
exceed these units.
