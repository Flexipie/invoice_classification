Dear Mr. Pepples:

Now that the seemingly interminable 1981 legislative
session has been recessed until the fall, we want to cordially
and enthusiastically thank you for the generous contribution
you made to ‘the success of our recent Seventh Annual Spring
Reception at Avery Pisher Hall.

By every standard of judgment--social, financial and
political--this was our greatest success. And while that is
encouraging and flattering to us and our 33 Senate Republican

‘colleagues, it was of more than symbolic importance if we are
to continue to display a statewide perspective that is fair,
responsible and logical.

Only by demonstrating that appeal will we continue to
roli up our majorities in the face of extraordinarily well
financed efforts of the opposition.

Your support has given us the first leg up on what may
well be the most challenging and difficult campaign since we
began our fund raising activities in this manner in 1975.

Over the next 15 months we are readying ourselves for a
very difficult onslaught, and your help this year is a key
factor in preparing us for this serious challenge.

On behalf of all 35 of our Republican Senators, we
extend our warmest thanks and kindest personal regards.
