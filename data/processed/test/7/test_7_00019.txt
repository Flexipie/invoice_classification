Per your request, this provides a rough draft of VANTAGE's first
quarter, 1986 Competitive Market Position,

« Performance

VANTAGE's first quarter Nielsen Food Store performance
exhibits stable share with a marginal share gain for the
quarter versus the year ago perio. VANTAGE's longer term
stabilizing trend is reflected in stable share performance in
the recent 12-month period (12 mos. ending Mar. 1985) versus
the year ago 12 month period. A key factor that continues to
be responsible for VANTAGE's stable performance is an
improving trend in the South Atlantic Sales Area and other
high development markets.

© Strategy

VANTAGE competes in the Moderation Segment, along with Merit,
and ig targeted against 25-49 year-old smokers (25-34 year-old
emphasis}. As a core volume Brand, VANTAGE's marketing
strategy Continues to focus on volume stabilization through
the execution of frequent carton-oriented promotions and the
overall concentration of marketing resources against high
development markets primarily in the South Atlantic end
Mid-Continent Sales Areas.
