RE:  Accountabitity for Coordination for Implementation
of Indirect Tar Control Measurements.

This is to establish the responsibitity for all efforts aimed
at the implementation of pressure drop measurements on the factory
floors which correlate with tar delivery and affect a higher fre-
quency of sampling and data input for tar control.

Charles L. Manning has been assigned to coordinate al] work
being done in Research, Development, Computer Science and other
areas aimed at correlating pressure drop measurements with tar
deliveries, organizing the quality assurance effort and imple-
menting the finished method as an indirect but much more rapid,
on-site measurement for tar control during production start-ups
and in regular production. In this capacity, Mr, Manning will
be expected to work closely with involved personnel in the areas
uentioned, particuarly with Analytical, Donestic Product Devetop-
ment and Physical Methods Development. The usual high level of
cooperation between the concerned operating areas will be needed
by Mr, Manning to meet his accountability and expeditious schedule.

Please inform the concerned personnel in your department of

this assignment and with the anticipation that Mr, Manning will be
seeking their help and counsel.
