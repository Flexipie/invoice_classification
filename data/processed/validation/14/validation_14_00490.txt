~ Dihydrocoumarin

Terminology

The systematic name for dihydrocoumarin is 1,2-benzodihydropyrone.
Common synonyms include 3,4-dihydrocoumarin and hydrocoumarin,

Sources

Commercial quantities of dihydrocoumarin are prepared by reduction
of coumarin using a nickel catalyst under pressure at 160-200°C
or in the presence of Pd-BaS0, in alcoholic solution (1), It has
been reported to occur naturafly in Melilotus officianalis (2),
deer tongue (3), and lavender oils (4).

Uses
