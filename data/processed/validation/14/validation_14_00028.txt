BLEND UNIFORMITY STUDIES

The major effort has been directed towards investigation of possible
methods that may be useful in determining blend uniformity.

Air separation of blend components has proved to be quite dependent
on particle size differences as well as differences in the density
between components, and therefore does not appear to be useful.

The determination of percent RCB in a blend by phosphorus analysis
is being investigated. Handblended samples containing various per-
centages of RCB in the blends have been analyzed for phosphorus,
