DEVELOPMENT PROJECT SUMMARY

MERIT - 100 mn

OBJECTIVE:

" Develop a 10-11 mg. Merit 100 mm cigarette having taste
characteristics comparable to higher delivery cigarettes.
ACCOMPLISHMENTS:

Selected Merit 100 candidate with additional Burley for HTI
testing versus True 100, Kent 100, Pall Mall 100, and Marlboro
100.

STATUS:

1. Completed RP* testing of initial Merit 100
candidate versus True 100, Kent 100, and PMI
Extra Milds. Versus True, Merit has more taste,
more tobacco-like flavor, and preferred; versus
Kent and PMI Extra Milds, no significant differences
noted by Combined Panel.

2. Reworked Merit 100 previously tested on RP° for
improved taste response with the following modifi-

cations:

- 1/2 Burley spray.

- Increased Burley in Blend.

+ Increased ET to raise tar/puff profile.
PLANS AND TIMETABLE:

Initiate HTI testing of improved Merit 100 2nd Quarter 1976.
