* Continued difficulties in titrating certain samples with the
Photovolt Aquatest II, plus increased sample load, resulted in the
purchase of a Photovolt Aquatast IV, an updated version of the II.
Initial studies has shown the Model IV to be fundamentally different
ia its method of determining water values. Discussions are in progress
