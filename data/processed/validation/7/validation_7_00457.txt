Please find attached, substitute pages with corrections for the Third
Quarter 1999 Sales Estimates for the Cigarette Industry report.

1) Lorillard’s nine month market share was incorrect. While volume
numbers were accurate, the company’s market share for the nine
months ended September 30, 1999 should have been 10.5% instead of
9.5%.

2) For the nine months, the “other companies” market share should have
been 2.1% instead of 1.9%. Volume numbers were correct.

We apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused.
