FIC Market Sample Pickup

After months of delay at: the FIC, progress toward a new
market sample pickup is now apparent. The FTC has sent out let-
ters to the companies requesting current brand information. If
all replies are received by December 1, the actual pickup could
start between December 15 and January 1. Historically, two to

three months have been required to complete a pickup.
