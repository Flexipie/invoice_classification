Subject: SPA Menthol Positioning Strategies

This summarizes Brand/Agency thinking on positioning strategy
alternatives for a menthol product.

I. Strategic options

There are six strategic options for introducing a menthol
product:

1. The simultaneous introduction of a regular/menthol
Premier family.

2, The sequential introduction of a Premier menthol line
extension, following the successful launch of the
non-menthol product.

3. The sequential introduction of a free-standing menthol
brand, following the successful launch of Premier
(non-menthol) «

4, The lead launch of Premier as a menthol product, followed
by the non-menthol as a line extension.

5. The lead launch of a free-standing Premier menthol,
followed by the introduction of a separate {new name)
non-menthol product.

6. The lead launch of a free-standing menthol (name other
than Premier), followed by the introduction of Premier
as a (separate) non-menthol.

From a positioning standpoint:
® Options 2 and 2 utilize the same family positioning {i.e.,

"world's cleanest cigarette"), The only difference is the
timing of the menthol introduction, which is a tactical

issue. Copy adjustments (e.g., "now available in
Menthol" or “available in regular and menthol") would be
minor.
