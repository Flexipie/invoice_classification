CAMEL Creative Direction

The purpose of this meme is to confirm recent direction dis-
cussed regarding CAMEL's major creative projects,

Interim Copy Pool

In general, the OOH materials recommended in our meeting
Monday, February 10, 1986 included the following elements:

@ Three models in action/smoking satisfaction shots.
@ Detailed, exotic backgrounds,

@ Package shots and brand name/style designations.
@ "It's A Whole New World” headline.

‘these elements worked well in the magazine ads, but produce
OOH boards which are judged to be too complicated to communi-
cate effectively under actual viewing conditions. Contribu~
ting to this complexity is the fact that the recommended
boards lacked the clean layout and ease of eye flow inherent
in current CAMEL World OOH materials. Therefore, it was
requested that the campaign be reduced to its most essential
elements, to allow for development of technically good OOH
boards which still efffectively communicate the intended
strategy.

In addition te simplifying the visuals, it was requested that
alternate layouts of the remaining elements be investigated to
further reduce the overall complex natura of the boards.
Finally, as we discussed subsequent to Monday's meeting, while
it is desirable to retain the headline, it may be dropped as
necessary at your discretion.
