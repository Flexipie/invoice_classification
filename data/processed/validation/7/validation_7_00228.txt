U,S.A.'s efforts to counter those attacks.

Let me first assure you that the news is not all
bad. Two major national public opinion polls have
found that an overwhelming majority of Americans --
both smokers and non-smokers -- believe that both

extremely well organized and dedicated to their

pursuit of a smokefree society. They are also very
