The tragic loss of Arthur Ashe has impacted those within the
tennis community as well as the scores of groups and
individuals Arthur touched during nis extraordinary life.
Arthur was well-known and well-respected as a result of his
many activities and roles =~ author, commentator, civil
xights activist, pro tennis player and ATP founder. Despite
his own tragedy, Arthur made a commitment during the last ten
months of his life to lead the fight against AIDS.

To pursue this mission, Arthur established the Arthur Ashe
Foundation for the Defeat of AIDS (AAFDA). At its inception
last summer, AAFDA embarked on a 15-month public education
and fundraising campaign. Its goal was to raise at least $5
million for AIDS research, education and patient care. with
a tremendous response from the tennis world, AAFDA is well on
its way to achieving this goal, but now more than ever, your
support is needed.

As a tournament sponsor and member of the tennis community,
please consider making a contribution in Arthur’s memory to
AAFDA. Your support for this cause will help educate the
public and assist in the search for a cure to defeat AIDS.
