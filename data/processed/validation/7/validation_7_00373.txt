1985 Second Quarter Report
Occupational Safety and Product Quality Review Committee

1, Subcommittee Status

A. Painting Procedure Subcommittee (W. D. Taylor,

Chairman)

1, Documents received from the National Paint:
Coating Association are being used to develop a
@raft of a Policy and Procedures.

Filter material has been added to the air diffusers
in affected plants to help reduce future paint
flaking/contamination problems.

Materials Approval Subcommittee (M. E. Miller,
Chairman)

1. A statement has been added to all contracts with
outside agencies, to all purchase orders and on all
local orders, covering the Company's position on
use of unacceptable materials.

2, A data base is being implemented in the procedures
for clearing materials for use.

3, Material Safety Data Sheets have been requested on
all purchases.

Insect Control Subcommittee (D. X. Isbister, Chairman)

Methaprene has been applied in #1 Factory, and results
to date are encouraging. A program is being designed
for total Company use.
