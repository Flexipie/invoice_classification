Dear Louis:

Ty Wilson asked me to investigate and respond to the matter
indicated in the recent letter from Mr. Kent B. Crane to
you regarding the Rational Rifle Association's magazine
and R. J, Reynolds.

Our Company's non-use of the National Rifle Association's
magazine should not be construed az any form of institu-
tional bias against the NRA itself.

The basic reason for not pursuing use of this magazine is
as follows:

The magazine was one of many we were evaluating, at the time,
to determine basic advertising value related to our brands*
needs.. It was decided this magazine was not to be included
as_a result of budget considerations and media values.

We certainly recognize the value of exposing our advertising
to the many enthusiasts of outdoor sports and hunting. This
is accomplished by our advertising representation in a large
number of outdoor/hunting magazines.

It is unfortunate that a representative of the publication
felt there was any form of approval given end then suddenly
withdrawn. We simply had not made a decision on the specific
publication per se.

Perhaps I'11 be seeing you personally again at an RJR function.
