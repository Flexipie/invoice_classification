SUBJECT: Establish @ Specification For Upper Reject Limit On % Stem Content
For G14

OBJECTIVE: To review and update specificalions on % Stem Content for G14 at
RES Tobacoo.

BACKGROUND: Referencing Procedure Note 93-21, an upper reject limit for
Objectionable stem content of 6.0% was proposed. The 6.0% was based on historical
Rotap Test Method data al Shed 119. change occurred in the standard test method
from the Rotep Method to the Cardwell Test Method. Therefore an investigation/review
of Historical (over time) CA capabilties data (CP & CPK data) has been completed and
utilized as a basis for developing % Stem Content upper reject limits,

PRODUCT IMPACT: Since no process change is being implemented, there should not
be any negative impact and should provide a higher quality produc! because of the
more sensitive test method,

PRODUCT DISPOSITION: Disposition or re-work procedures for CRES product
exceeding these limits are that the material will be rerun by introducing it before the
tower separator to drop out additional material to meet the specification. Material may
also be diluted with low stem materiat to meet specification.

RISK ASSESSMENT: We do not see any risk associated with the proposal at this
time, in fact, the more sensitive setup of the Cardwell Test Method will provide a more
consistent product.

RE ‘TION: It is recommended that this specification be accepted by the
PAG Committee, This procedure note will be reviewed in light of on-going process
capability studies upon the completion of a 90 day review period.
