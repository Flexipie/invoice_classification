SALES/MARKETING

* Inthe state of NEVADA, Senator Ernie Adler has requested a Bill Draft
Request proposing to make it unlawful for minors to possess tobacco
products. This is the first BDR we have seen in Nevada that would affect the
industry.

LOGAL

* On duly 1, in Mesa, ARIZONA, Proposition 200 went into effect. The new law
bans smoking in all public places, including workplaces, bars and restaurants
in Mesa, although bars may seek an exemption. This week a Mesa business
owner and other individuals filed suit against the smoking ban initiative. No
date has been set for court action. In a separate but similar suit, plaintiffs who
are owners of various non-class-6 licensed businesses and restaurants are
scheduled for an initial hearing today, 8/29, before the Superior Gourt of
Maricopa County.
