SUBJECT: UTI UPDATE (GP2SC)

OBJECTIVE: To review and update specifications and procedures for UTI to be
produced at CRES Tobacco Company.

DISCUSSION: The following is an update to the UTI hase spexificalion to compile all
changes into one note.

Moisture: UTI tobacco product produced by CRES Tobacco Company will have
a moisture target of 12.5% with a range of 11.5 to 13.5%. ‘The product will be
stored under controlled conditions at 60% R.H,

Total Stem: UTI product produced by CRES Tobacco Company will have an
upper limit of 1.5% (PN 96-09}.

Particle Size Distribution: The upper limit for particle size in finished GP2SC is
a 5% maximum smaller han .033 in. (20 mesh) based on the average of three
individual tests per bulker (PN 98-15).

RISK ASSESSMENT: We do not see any risk associated with the proposal.

RECOMMENDATION: Adopt the updated UTI specifications for current operations at
CRES Tobacco Company.
