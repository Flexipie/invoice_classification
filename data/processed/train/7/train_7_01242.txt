RE: CAMEL Regular Tar Reduction

Plans are to reduce the tar of CAMEL Regular to 21mg.
about midyear, 1980. This will involve a blend change
and paper change. However, to avoid obsoleting paper in
inventory, it is expected that the first phase with be to
utilize current 855 paper with the new blend until the” _
paper stock is exhausted, at which time the new paper —
will be substituted, The first phase is expected to re-
duce tar one or two mg. The decision on the interim
change should be made May 9, 1980,
