Since you were unable to attend our Industries Board meeting
on June 1? when the newest member of the VANTAGE brand family,
VANTAGE Ultra Lights Menthol, was distributed, I have enclosed
cartons of this new product for you. This product is being
introduced nationally by Reynolds Tobacco on July 5.

VANTAGE Ultra Lights Menthol, offered in 85mm and 100mm styles
with 5 mg “tar”, is positioned to compete in the ultra-low "tar"
menthol categories.

The 1970 introduction of VANTAGE heralded Reynolds Tobacco's
future leadership in the low "tar" market. The 1979 intro-
duction of VANTAGE Ultra Lights helped solidify our ultra-low
“tar” position.

The results are impressive. In 1981, we posted the Largest
share -- 29.04 percent -- of the ultra-low "tar" market. Our
35.95 percent share of the menthol segment last year was also
tops in the industry.

The expertise gained in these categories is embodied in VANTAGE
Ultra Lights Menthol. This product should further strengthen
Reynolds Tobacco's leadership of the ultra-low “tar” and
menthol categories.

We hope you will enjoy the newest product from America's
leading tobacco company and will share our pride in promoting
this product,
