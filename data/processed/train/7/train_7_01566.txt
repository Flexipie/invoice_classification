Sensory Evaluation of Menthol

On comparing the aromas of two different source synthetic
nenthols and one natural menthol, it was found that all three
compounds possessed different aromas. In addition, one of
the synthetic manthols was found to possess an off-aroma. To
evaluate the impact of these aroma differences, three
cigarette products were made in the R&D Plant and top dressed
respectively with the three different sources of menthol at
the same lével. The Menthol Discrimination Panel conducted
suoking evaluations of these three preducts and found no
significant difference between the natural menthol and the
two synthetic menthols even when a single menthol source was
compared against another. ‘These results demonstrate that the
off-aroma found in one of the synthetic menthols aid not
generate any taste differences in the final cigarette
products. They also confirm the earlier findings that there
is no difference between natural and synthetic menthol when
used in the top dressing of a cigarette.
