COMPOUNDS NORMALLY FOUND IN CASED CIGARETTE BLENDS. AVERAGE FID AREA COUNTS
‘OF IDENTIFIED COMPOUNDS ARE ATTACHED. IN THIS SAMPLE THE MAJOR COMPONENT IS:
ACETIC ACID, ACCOUNTING FOR 30% OF THE TOTAL FID AREA.
