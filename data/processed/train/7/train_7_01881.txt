Dear (Salutation):

Thank you for your letter. We are pleased to have this
opportunity to respond to your concerns regarding Virginia
Slims’ sponsorship of women's tennis.

Virginia Slims tennis is one of the biggest success stories
in the history of corporate sports sponsorship. When Philip
Morris U.S.A. first began sponsoring women's tennis more than
20 years ago, the sport was merely a side-show to
professional men's tennis, Today, thanks in large part to
Virginia Slims’ sponsorship, women's tennis is a world class
sport, providing millions of avid fans with the opportunity
to see the best players in the world compete for top prizes
and international acclaim.

Now that tobacco use has become more controversial, there are
these who suggest we should disassociate women's tennis from
the Virginia Slims name. We do not agree.

From the beginning of its association with women's tennis,
Philip Morris has never asked any player to use or endorse
its products, including cigarettes, beer or food. Tobacco
remains a legal product, and using tobacco a legitimate
lifestyle choice for informed adults, We do not believe that
cigarette advertising or promotion, including the Virginia
Slims tennis tournaments, encourages non-smokers of any age
to begin smoking, and all available studies support this
view.

Thank you for taking the time te share your concerns with us.
I hope I have provided you with a full and satisfactory
explanation of our position on this issue.
