The following provoses that we update our information
regarding the attitudes of RJR employees toward smoking

Background

In November, 1979, a study was conducted among RIR employees
to identify their attitudes toward smoking and related
social and legislative issues, This study identified a
strong base of positive attitudes--especially within RJR
Tobacco and secondly among RJR Archer /Industries.

The study also found that most of the information available to
these employees came from the public media and was anti-smoking
in nature. As a result, a pro-tobacco information program

was initiated which was delivered to RJR employees through
Company newsletters such as the Caravan and RJR World.

There is currently a need to update our information on
employee attitudes toward smoking related issues/Icgislation
and to assess the effectiveness of the pro-tobacco information
program. This assessment needs to be diagnostic and directed
toward further refinements/improvements of programs
