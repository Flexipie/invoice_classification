SUBJECT: PRODUCT ANALYTICALS

Per your request, following is an update of product analytical
results.

100M, VFBS, VULF85S

As noted in my memo of 6/5, these products had been running
high in tar {with the exception of 100M, which was at the upper
end of Action Limits at 9.9}. Subsequent monitoring yielded
the following: .

> V100M (Action Limits 8.7-9.9): Since the 9.9 reading, V100M
analyticals have been at lower tar levels of between 9.3 and
9.6.

~ VP8S (Action Limits 8.3-9.5}: Since manufacturing began
producing the 70MI product, tar levels have been
consistently within action limite, running between 8.8 and
9.2,

- WULF85 [Action Limits 4.8-5.8}: After R&D adjusted the
Taser setting following a high (5.9) reading on 5/15, this
product has also been within limits at 5.3-5.6,

Remaining Brand Styles: Remaining styles have been running

within action Timits with the exception of VMB5 (AL: B.5-9,7)
which from 5/21-23 posted a high tar level of 10.2. Subsequent
yeads indicated that the product is now running within action
limits (June average: 9.5; July average: 9.3). With the new
70MI prototype scheduled for Phase III on 8/20, we can expect
product analyticals on this product starting 8/27, and should
see more consistent tar levele in the future,

Melecde
