Dear Bill:

City Parks Foundation is proud to serve as the official charity of the Virginia
Slims Championships, the largest women’s sporting event in the world, As a board
member of City Parks Foundation, I am pleased to invite you to join us as 2 supporter of
inner-city recreation programs for city youth and of City Parks Foundation at this
prestigious and fun event,

For the past three years we have been the chosen charity of the top women’s
tennis players. Over $250,000 has been raised for the expansion of free tennis and other
recreational programs for city kids throughout the five boroughs.

Scheduled for November 14-20, 1994, the 1994 Championships will be dramatic.
Martina Navratilova will be making her final appearance as a professional tennis player.
We will also be watching and tracking the rapid emergence of Mary Pierce as a dynamic
player on the circuit.

By choosing to support City Parks Foundation in this way, you will receive a
prime courtside box for the full seven days of the tournament, During the week we will
he-ffering our City Parks Foundation box participants a social buffet dinner at Madison
Square Garden prior to the matches.

City Parks Foundation raises funds for park improvements and recreational
programs throughout the city. These are invaluable to the lives of all New Yorkers,
especially our city youth, This is one of our most important events of the year and we
hope we can count on your support. Attached you will find a full description of the
package. Please review the materials and let us know if you can join us.
