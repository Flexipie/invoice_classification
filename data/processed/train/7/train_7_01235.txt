SUBJECT: Use of Wikoff Color Corp, Inks at Winston Printing

Scientific Affairs has reviewed the formulations for the following Wikoff Color Corp.
(Fort Mill, SC) inks to be used by Winston Printing for offset printing cartons and crush-
proof boxes for export:

‘Winston Red G-1570
Salem Gold G-2237

These inks are approved from a toxicological point-of-view. We are not familiar with the
regulatory status in other countries of the components of these inks, however they would be
acceptable for the domestic market. The formulas for these inks are in the confidential files
of Scientific Affairs.
