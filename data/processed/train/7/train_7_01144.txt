This memo reports topline results of the BY 85mm product test
fielded through M/A/R/C on April 25, 1979,

BACKGROUND: Project By's objective is to provide a new non-menthol
Sigarette which meets the product and user imagery wants of Segment C
type smokers. Results of the 1977 Segmentation Study have indicated
that these smokers want a cigarette which provides superior satis-
faction and taste, is low in tar and nicotine, and is supported ‘
through an appealing masculine user image. Current plans are to
introduce BY in both 85mm soft pack and 80mm box styles; product
development on the box style is scheduled to be initiated upon
determination of the @5mm product.
