Re: MEDIA DEPARTMENT)REORGANIZATION

This document is extremely sensitive. Please limit its circulation
to people who have a need to know.

Recommendation

We recommend that the Company establish a totally self-sufficient
Media Department internally. This Department will handle all media
planning, execution and administration for our cigarette brands and
the planning for STP brands using broadcast media. Broadcast media
execution will be the responsibility of the assigned agency.

In net, this internal media organization will replace the need for
media services currently provided by the agencies assiqned to our
cigarette brands.

Rationale

Conversion to an internal media operation will provide the following
benefits:

Immediate reduction in media operating costs estimated to be
$1.2MM in Year I. In Year Il, the savings are estimated to
be $2,810,000,

2. Increased understanding of Company and individual brand media
needs coupled with faster turn-around time.

3. Increased control of long-term media costs.

4, Administrative efficiencies arising from centralization and
use of consolidated EDP systems,

Better quality control over media personnel and individual
performance.

Net, an internal media operation will increase efficiency and result

in a higher quality of media service from both a Corporate learning
as well as an individual brand perspective.

Impact

If Management concurs with this recommendation, it will have a major
impact on both RJRT and our agencies.
