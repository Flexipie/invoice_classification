Requested Information on Components of Cigarette Papers

‘As per your request, listed below are the maximun percentages of the
compounds designed into our current grades of cigarette papers:
WHITE CIGARETTE PAPER GRADES

1. Cellulose Fiber - 80%

2. Caleium Carbonate ~ 40x

3A. Sodium Citrate and/or Potassium Citrate - 3.08

OR

3B. Mono Ammonium Phosphate — 1.0%
‘The above is true for all of our white cigarette papers. For the
colored papers, the above is also true with the addition of the
following dyes:
WORE. LIGHT 100% and MORE 120¢s

1, Direct Orange 102.1 - 8.0%

2. Direct Yellow 64 ~ 2.0%

3. Direct Blue 15 = 2.0%
‘The Black Overprint on MORE 120's

1. Carbon Black Pigment/Ethy) Cellulose Solvent Ink - 10%
2. Maleic Resin (residual from Carbon Black Pigment ) ~ 1%

BOOKLET PAPERS.
1, Cellulose Fiber ~ 90%
2, CaCO3 - 10%

3. TH02 (only in one grade) - < 2%
4, Sodium Citrate and/or Potassium Citrate ~ 3.05
