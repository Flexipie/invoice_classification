Carton Exposure In Concept/Product Test

Subject

We could do e carton extended use test in the concept product
test, but MDD does not recommend using the C/P test to try to
assess extended use (see attached). Their position is that the
TPT is the place where this showld be assessed.

Based on a request from RAK on extended use testing in general,
an experimental study was developed to determine if there is any
other viable means of assessing extended use other than TPT.

thet work is in the field and will be complete by year-end. MDD
plans to present the results to RAK January 12, and I asked

Bill Whitlatch that we be made aware of the results prior to that
time go we won't be put in the position of knowing less than he
does about the issue. Bill agreed to do so.

If an acceptable alternative to TPT can be found for extended use
testing, we can incorporate it into our testing.
