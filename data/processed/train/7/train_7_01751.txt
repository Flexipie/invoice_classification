RR Interoffice Hemorandum

Subject: Anmoniation of G-7 Extract Date: May 11, 1987
Tos Mr. W. D. Young From: Or. Scott Appleton
Conclusion:

Scientific Affairs approves amnoniated G-7 extract, (at a 3% application
rate) for further review by the RJT Human Research Review Committee.

Background:

Scientific Affairs has previously approved sensory panel evaluation of
tobacco sheet products containing G-7 extract which had been anmoniated at a
1.5% application rate.

The smoke data you provided me indicates that extract ammoniated at a 3%
application rate has essentially no effect on the parameters measured compared
to the 1.5% ammoniated extract. In addition, smoke ammonia and nicotine
delivery from sheet containing the 3% ammoniated extract was tess than that

from conventional G-7A.
