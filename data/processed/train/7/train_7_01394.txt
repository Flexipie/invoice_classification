Waterglass Study

The results of the effects of waterglass added to the heat sourca on Alpha product
performance were reported to appropriate personnel 1/4/90, Products tested
included a control with no waterglass added, a praduct with 0.2 mg waterglass
added, and a product with 0.4 mg waterglass added. Resvits Indicated that product
performance was severely altered by the addition of waterglass at any level that it is
not a viable option for use as a heat source retention agent as it is currently used.
