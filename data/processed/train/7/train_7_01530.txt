Conclusions

= SPORTS PAGE is a unique media form that was designed to
deliver tonnage against a blue collar male 18-34 audience,

- AS currently structured, the SPORTS PAGE contract does
not provide flexibility in terms of geographical
distribution, establishment type, or placement of location
within an establishment.

= Based on consumer research measuring WINSTON communication
levels, SPORTS PAGE has the capability to be both an
efficient and effective medium. We can only conclude that if
SPORTS PAGE were operating at maximum effectiveness this
program's value to the Corporation would be greatly
increased.

$2,000.0M penalty for early cancellation of the program,
Currently $2,334.0M is budgeted for SPORTS PAGE for the
period May-December 1984. This option would return $334.0M
to the WINSTON budget for other uses. This would also
result in relieving a commitment of §9,368.0M for the
remainder of the life of this contract.
