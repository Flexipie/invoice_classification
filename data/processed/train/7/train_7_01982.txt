Subject: — STF-5061

All components of STF-5061 are approved for use in the United States and
Germany. Each of the components is FEMA GRAS, natural or nature identical, on the
Industry List of 599 ingredients recently published (over half currently used by RIR) and
reported to be used in foods and/or beverages and/or candies, etc. (although not all have
FDA GRAS cltations).

Regulatory status has not been verified in other countries, such as the United
Kingdom, France, Belgium and Switzerland. Verification of regulatory status worldwide
is time consuming and since interest from Project GTC has been expressed for the United
States and Germany at this time, these countries have been checked first. Should interest
for other countries become apparent, please advise and regulatory status will be checked

at that time.

Please advise if anything else is needed.
