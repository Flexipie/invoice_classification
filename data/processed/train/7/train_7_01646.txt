BACKGROUND

Several changes occurred during May affecting not only model input/output, but
implications as well. Our target inventory duration policy was changed in an effort to
more etosely reflect actual inventories/usage/predicted availability, These adjustments
were approved by the Ingredient Utilization and Leaf and Blends Committees in Mey.
Additionally, our G71-3 formulas were altered to (1) prevent unnecessary storage of
excess materials (i.e, cigarette scrap) and (2) increase utilization of ingredients which
exist in surplus, Previous IDM updates have identified flue-cured stems, burley scrap
and cigarette scrap as materials in this surplus category. Therefore, formula ranges
changed in the following manner: Stem/fiue-cured increase of 5%, burley decrease of
5%; Scrap/cigarette increase of 2%, burley increase of 1%, flue-cured decrease of 3%.

‘The aforementioned changes have, collectively, enabled us to better meet target
durations thus, creating a more balanced inventory as will be evidenced by this report.
