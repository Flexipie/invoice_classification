ADVANCED PROCESS TECHNOLOGY

* Alternate Forms of Tobacco - Screw Extruded Shapes

Fourth Test Run Results in a 24% Decrease in Product
Density

The fourth experimental run on the twin screw extruder
has been completed. ‘The object of the test was to begin
attempts to reduce the rod density and increase the
effective filling capacity of extruded shapes. The ex-
periment was also designed to compare extruded shapes
dried in a conventional hot air type dryer versus a
microwave dryer. One percent, by weight, corn starch
was added to the extruder feed to help reduce rod densi-
ty. Product dried in the conventional oven showed 16.63
decrease in rod density. Microwave dried product re~
sulted in a 24% decrease in rod density.
