Gentlemen:

My name is Louise Murphy and I am a purchaser of Salem Ultra 100s. The

last carton I purchased was terrible. In every pack three or four cigarettes
were not made properly. The Filters were loose in the paper binding. The
cuter layer of paper was krinkled and they were hard to smoke and very un-
confortable. I spend a lot of money on cigarettes as I stoke three packs a
day and I feel cheated. Please consider telling your processers to be more
careful. We habitual smokers need you to give us quality. God knows we

pay for it.

1 am sorry if 1 sound critical, but I feel if I say something now I might
avoid future disappointments.

Sincerely,

He np

Loulse Murphy
