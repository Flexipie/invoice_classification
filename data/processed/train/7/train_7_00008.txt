INTER-OFFICE MEMORANDUM

October 6, 1988

70: Dr. G. R. Di Marco
SUBJECT: Weekly Highlights
Process Technology & Development
I. PROCESS DEVELOPMENT :

© AVOCA Propane Pilot Plant Check-out Completed
Hydraulic and pneumatic pressure testing have been con-
pleted for check-out of the AVOCA Pilot Plant. Minor
changes were made to correct leaks. This completes this
check-out of the equipment. Instrumentation for the
quick analysis of residual propane in expanded tobacco
is at AVOCA. During the week of October 3, the system
will be charged with propane, and tebacco expansion tri-
als will begin.

© Whole Leaf Process Development

During September testing of a final blend containing .
whole leaf, it was found that the long strands of cut
filler were not significantly improved over the same
blend containing strip. Detailed analysis of the pro-
cessing found that stem separation at cutting moisture
{198) gave an improvement compared with separation at
final cut filler moisture (12.5%). It was also found
that measurement of the +3 mesh sieve fraction gives a
better characterization of the processes. With these
changes, WLP is now showing slightly longer cut filler
than blends vith strip. This approach will be continued
as the cut filler is evaluated in making tests.
