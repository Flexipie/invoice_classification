Warning Labels

The enclosed publication entitled "Smoking Issues
Status Book - Constituents/Warning Labels, Volume I,”
1990 Edition, published by The International Tobacco
Information Centre, sets forth, as of June 30, 1990,
under the Tab “Warning Labels” those countries in shich
warning labels are required on cigarette packages and
the text thereof. Later information indicates that
updating changes are required. As of this writing,
contrary to what is stated, Bolivia, Nigeria and Turkey
do require warning labels.
