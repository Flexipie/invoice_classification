strategies we plan to pursue in our upcoming creative

Current Positioning/Copy Strategy
© Positioning Statement

"MORE is a visibly different cigarette for smokers who want
to stand out and be noticed. These smokers are primarily
women aged 25 ~ 49, and MORE is the only brand that allows
them to make a dramatic visual impression because of its
visually distinctive products."

© Focus of Sale

"MORE 120s is a cigarette whose distinctive length and color
provide women with a means to express their self-confidence
and independence.”

Recommended Positioning

"MORE is a visibly different cigarette for women who want to
stand out and be noticed by expressing their unique personal
style and character, These smokers are primarily 25 - 49, and
MORE is the only brand that allows them to make a positive visual
impression because of its visually distinctive products.”
