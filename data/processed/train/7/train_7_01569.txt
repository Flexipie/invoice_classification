Vitality

Versus the current campaign, the vitality campaign was seen as
younger, more alive, bolder, more modern, free spirited,
active and sportier, However, it's appeal was primarily
limited to younger smokers and it provided weak category
communication. Most importantly, while it was viewed
positively, it was not viewed as unique or distinctive from
current category offerings/too much like Virginia Slims.

Allure

Allure was seen as very feminine, sexy and a fashion ad. A .-
brand smoked by Joan Collins. However, it was also seen as
calm, non-active, and not fun loving. The more sensuous
executions were also viewed by some participants as
distasteful.

Ingenuity

Ingenuity, with its split visuals, was seen as confusing by
some participants. The campaign was seen as private, pensive,
alone and subtle. It was too elegant/serious and many
participants could not relate to it. It did not interact with
their lives or fantasies.
