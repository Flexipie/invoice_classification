April 18, 1984

Dr. G. R. Di Marco

Brand R&D Weekly Status Report

NEW BRANDS

* Project DS.

The regional launch of new DORAL (0S) is scheduled for a Hay 1%, 1984 D-T-
S. The regional load quantities of each of the four styles have been pro—

duced.

Both of the 85mm styles achieved the FIC “Tar” objective. The FIC ‘Tar’
data on the 100an styles have not been received.

A meeting was held on Friday April 13, 1984 with representatives of Brands
RaD, Quality Assurance, Manufacturing and Brands Naintenance in atten-
dance. The discussion in this meeting focused on the "loose-ends/vold-
ends" encountered during production of the 100mm non-menthol style and the
differences between Factory #64, where the two menthol styles are pro-
duced, and Whitaker Park, shere the two non-menthol styles are produced.
Testing of the DS blend with lowered pack moisture and no added shorts has
been done. Improvement of the "Loose-ends/void-ends” phenomenon on the
100mm non-menthol style was observed. Further testing is underway to
determine vhy this problem exists only on the 100m non-menthol style.

Samples of the 100nm non-menthol style obtained from the CDC have been re-
evaluated for product quality. This product was within acceptable limits

and will be shipped.
