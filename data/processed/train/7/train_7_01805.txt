INTEROFFICE MEMORANDUM

To: Menthol Task Force FROM: W. J. Casey

RE: Various Carbon Plug Length DATE: January 17, 1989
8-12 mn

Objective

Using one lot of carbon paper rods (#8-067C) measure delivery
affect from changing the length of the carbon plug incorporated
into Premier.

Experiment

Make MEP’s from 30 nm polypropylene plus either 8, 10 or 12 mm of
carbon paper plug. Attach front end piece obtained from
cigarette 85-0338 via lab. Tipper laser to 22% air dilution.
Condition cigarettes at 75°F/40% relative humidity.

Results

Attached are results from machine smoking for 4 Premier
cigarettes:

1. Reference Cigt. #8-0333
2. Carbon Paper Plug 6 mm
3. Carbon Paper Plug 10 mm
4. Carbon Paper Plug 12 m

There was no significant difference in WIPM, nicotine or glycerin
delivery between the three test cigarettes.
