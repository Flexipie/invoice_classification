Dear Terese:

I enjoyed the opportunity to talk over matters of
mutual concern with you the other day.

As you will remember, after the scientific Advisory
Council to the National Institute on Drug Abuse took its
precipitous January action to recommend prohibition of
Cigarette advertising, we asked NIDA Director William
Pollin to bring about a reconsideration.

He has just replied and we have responded to that
reply. Copies of the letters are enclosed for your
information.

I would like now to urge strongly that you make your
views on tnis issue known directly to Dr. Pollin. It seems
quite clear that his Advisory Council members were poorly
informed as a basis for their action end their action does
not appear to be in the interest of the public, our
industry or yours.

1 will be most grateful to learn of any followup steps
you choose to take.

Cordially,
