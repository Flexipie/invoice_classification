Brands R&D Weekly Highlights

CAMEL/GALI } x7 nt

The following eight (8) new/improved products in the
CAMEL/SALEM/MAGNA brand development group will begin production
during the weeks of 01/15-01/22/90:

. CAMEL - FC (2)

Manufacturing start-up is on schedule for Project Fc, a
CAMEL line extension designed to deliver smooth tobacco
taste via a wide gauge (27.0mm circumference) cigarette.
Maker/packer trials this week and next are preceding
official production, set for 02/05/90.

CAMEL FC, targeted at virile male young adult smokers, wiil
be available in FF and FFLT 79nm box styles. DTS for
regional introduction into 24% of the U.S. is 05/07/90.

. SALEM - MR (4)

Manufacturing start-up for Project MR, the lower menthol
version of the key SALEM styles, will begin this week. Test
market DTS for the new SKS, 5100, SL85, and SL100 is
03/05/90 in the Cleveland/Toledo area.

+ SALEM - SB (1)
Manufacturing start-up for Project SB, a cork-tipped FF

SALEM box product, is scheduled for 01/22/90. Regional DTS
is 04/02/90 into 22% of the U.S.
