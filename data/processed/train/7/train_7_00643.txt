March 19, 1960

To: Mr. H. J. Lees

RE: PRELIMINARY DEVELOPMENT PLAN FOR DORAL 100mm BRAND

Attached is the preliminary development plan for a DORAL 100mm
brand. This recommendation contains only the overall strategic
thinking (i.e. brand positioning, name, packaging, and copy
strategy, etc.) for a national introduction of a 100mm, 5 mg.,
DORAL "Ultra" (filter and:menthol) brand targeted solely to 100mm
female smokers. DORAL "Ultra" would replace Parent DORAL on
retail shelves and be the only style to receive marketing support,
The Focus of sale for this positioning is ae follows:

DORAL “Ultra” 100's is a new, 100mm, ultra low tar cigarette that
delivers a superior smoking experience required by contemporary,
intelligent women who lead active ana rewarding lives.

The basic premise of the positioning is to utilize imagery (unlike
the current ULT numbers/excellent taste advertising) to address
the desired perceptions of female smokers (i.e. intelligent,
contemporary, out-going women who enjoy smoking and the active
lives they live). “The positioning ties into a product which is a
100mm, ultra low tar that delivers a superior smoking experience
for women who want a light, mild, smooth tasting cigarette and
make their brand selection on these taste characteristics, not
specific tar numbers.

Specific media and sales promotion plans will be submitted after
Management agreement is received regarding the attached strategic
plans,

Please advise if you have any questions or problems.
