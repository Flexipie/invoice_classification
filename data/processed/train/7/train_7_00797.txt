Subject: Promotion of Mr. Mitchell F, stiles

Date: September 1, 1992

Please join me and Mr. Riley Davis in congratulating Mr. Mitchell F.
Stiles on his promotion to Associate R&D Technologist, effective September
1, 1992.

Mr. Stiles's work on developing and applying methods for the analysis
of nicotine, its metabolites or other compounds in biological fluids is
crucial to the mission of the Biobehavioral Division. The data from these
analyses are key endpoints to the majority of the studies that are
performed in the Division.

Each year, the Biobehavioral Division has increased the number and
complexity of the studies that it performs and each year more support is
provided to other R&D units. Major projects that have benefitted from this
work include Prenier, XB, Nicotine Rs, and the ETS Japanese Spouse Pairs
Study, among others. Increases in throughput have been made while headcount
was reduced, indicating that efficiency is increasing. This is que, in
large part, to the consistently high level of performance provided by Mr.
Stiles.

Mr, Stiles has consistently demonstrated perseverance, high standards,
and a willingness to accept change and unplanned extra work.
