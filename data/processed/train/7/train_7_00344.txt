SALEM King/C30A - Carbowax Test

Previous SED tests on menthol and non-menthol Cigarettes indicated that
‘the presence of carbowax in the filter reduced the intensity of many
harshness related attributes. Testing of C30A in the blend of menthol
cigarettes showed a C30A/menthol interaction such that C30A and low
Jevel menthol (0.35%) increased tobacco taste.

In order to act on the development direction for FFM products, which
ncludes increasing tobacco taste while reducing harshness, products
were made this week in the R&D Piant to evaluate the use of carbowax in
the filter (0, 2.5, & 8%) and C30A in the blend (0, 13, & 26%} of a FFM
cigarette at three menthol levels (0.25, 0.35, & 0.45%). These
prototypes wil) be tested on internal diagnostic panels at 1, 3, and 5
months of age. Initial results on the fresh (1 nonth old) prototypes
should be available by late June.

Learning from this study will be applied to the development of FFM
products as well as the development of prototypes for Project MR.
