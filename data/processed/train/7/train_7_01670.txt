March 10, 1959

Mr, Julius Strauss
General Cigar Co., Inc,
485 Lexington Avenue
New York 17, New York

Dear Mr. Strauss:

Lam glad to learn through a letter just
received from Mr. F. G. Bodde that he and I
are to have the pleasure of meeting with you
in New York during his forthcoming visit to
this country.

Mr. Bodde informs me that you plari to
leave New York for Chicago on Saturday, April 4,
and in the circumstances I wonder if Friday,
April 3, would be convenient for you to meet

with us,

If this date is agreeable to you please let
me know the time of day you would suggest that
we plan our meeting.

With ali good wiahee, I am

Very sincerely yours,

wi, S. Cutehins
