QUALITY CONTROL OF PEPPERMINT AND SPEARMINT OILS

A neeting was held with Planters Life Savers QA Sensory to
finalize a protocol that will be used to qualify their ship-
ments of peppermint and spearmint oils from their various
suppliers. Flavor Division staff had generated 21 descrip-
tive terms and samples, submitted by Planters, will be rated
and then compared to a qualified referenca sample to reach to
a consensus of either acceptable or unacceptable. A total of

about 100 samples of freshly distilled oils will he screened
in the next two months,
