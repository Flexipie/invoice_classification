Dear TAN Volunteer:

‘The Ormond Beach City Council has scheduled a hearing

on a proposed clean indoor air act. While we have yet
to see a copy of this proposal, we know that clean indoor
air act measures in other jurisdictions throughout the
country have sought to restrict or even prohibit smoking
in numerous business establishments and places of employ-
ment.

I need your help in objecting to this proposal which is
unnecessary and unenforceable. If you agree that we need
less government interference and less regulation in our
daily lives, I urge you to write letters ta the Mayor and
members of the City Commission expressing your oppesition
to this measure.

T have enclosed a List of points that you can rake (in your
own words, please) in your letter: also cnclosed are the
names, addresses, and telephone numbers of the Commission
members, In addition, it would be extremely helpful if you
could attend the public hearing on May31 at 7:30 p.m. in
the City Commission Meeting Roo, Ormond Beach City Hall,
22 South Beach Street, Ormond Beach, Florida.

The time has come to stand up and be counted among those who
are willing to defend actively our industry and our individ-
ual freedoms. I am sure I can count on you to work with me
on this project. 1 stand ready to assist you in any way

necessary.
Sincerely,
Jack Shoesaker
Area Director
