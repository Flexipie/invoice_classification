INTEROFFICE MEMORANDUM

November 17, 1986

To: C, T. Mansfield

Re: Reduction of Carbon Monoxide in Cigarette Smoke
(Your memorandum of 9 October to G. R. Di Marco)

Cliff, thanks for sending me a copy of the above memo. It is
a very good summary of the approaches which have been used to
modify CO delivery. In reading the memorandum, I did not get a
sense for the magnitude of the RJRT R&D effort on CO (gas phase)
reduction which took place in the late sixties and early
Seventies. This effort was much more extensive than the work
which took place after the Gori article. It included laboratory
evaluation of literature/patent proposals, an active program with
SRI and other external sources of potential removal agents, and
an in-house synthesis program for active filtration agents.

Thanks again for the report. Let me know df you and Alan
find any promising leads at Battelle.
