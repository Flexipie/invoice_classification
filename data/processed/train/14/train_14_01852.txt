IIT. SMOKER SATISFACTION

1, Cardiac rhythm and finger-tip temperature were measured

on NEF smokers whose puff count varied between 3-4-5-6
and 10 puffs.

2. Cardiac hythm increased during smoking up to the 4th

or Sth puff then the level reached was maintained to the
end of the cigarette. 1 the subject stopped smoking,
cardiac rhythm decreased to the initial level after a

25 to 30 minute interval.

Finger-tip temperature decreased while the subject was
smoking and then returned slowly to a normal temperature.
Tt seems that the vasoconstriction is well maintained

after the effect of the nicotine on cardiac rhythm has
ended,
