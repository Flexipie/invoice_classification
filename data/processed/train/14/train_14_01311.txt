TITLE

Basic Research on Nicotine Receptors
PRINCIPAL INVESTIGATOR

Dr. Jean-Pol Tassin
Department of Neuropharmacology at the Collage de France, Paris.

OBJECTIVE

To explain the facilitation of the learning process as described by other
investigators.

PRINCIPAL INVESTIGATOR - BACKGROUND

Dr. Tassin is the head of a research group in the department which is run by Prof,
Jacques Glowinski. This group is well known for the high quality of their research on
receptors in the brain and has published numerous papers in reputed scientific
journals, Dr, Tassin was the head of a subgroup to the committee which was
appointed by the French government to study tobacco and related problems ("Hirsch
Commission"), IAM was the representative of the Industry in the same group.
RESPONSIBLE FOR $8q: 1AM.

COST AND DURATION

The research project was started in 1989 with an annual contribution of FFR
350,000. For this year, the contribution was reduced by 50% (FFR 175,000-), but a
continuation for 1995 - possibly at 100% - is strongly advisable.

MAN-HOURS SPENT FOR THIS PROJECT

Contacts as warranted, but at least quarterly visits.
