STUDY

PAPIN 8500

Objective

The purpose of this study was to investigate the influence of
cigarette papers on smoke deliveries in order to take this into
account in the existing simulation models.

To update a catalogue containing the relative smoke yields of
papers.

status
Catalogue : a new series of prototypes, with currently used and
also special papers, was made and analysed in the smoking
laboratory.

The results were introduced in the existing catalogue and an
updated list of all the papers in decreasing order of the
influence on the smoke yields was established and delivered to
the project leaders of the department.

Sidestream : a series of recently developed special papers for
Sidestream reduction was submitted by Mauduit.

Prototypes were made with these papers and analysis of the
sidestream smoke was performed in the research laboratories.

Puff per puff : in order to investigate the influence of the
cigarette paper on the puff per puff profile, several Papin

Prototypes were chosen for TPM puff per puff analysis in the
research laboratories.
