ASSISTANCE ‘TO EGYPT (RPJ)

A visit was paid to the Talbiae factory in Cairo, Egypt to
perform a quality audit. The opportunity was also taken to make
the personnel feel more concerned about quality. Various
adjustments were made to the makers and packers, resulting in a
decrease in the number and severity of defects. The fitters and
operators were given precise information on PM quality
requirements, with emphasis on the defects commonly encountered
in the factory, Several propositions were made to AOD (1).
