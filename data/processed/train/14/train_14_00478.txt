TITLE

Neuronal Cholinergic-Nicotinic Receptors And
Alzheimer's Disease

PRINCIPAL INVESTIGATOR

Prof. Francesco Clementi
institute of Pharmacology, University of Milan.

OBJECTIVE

To identify the specific genetic subunits whose expression is affected in Alzheimer's
disease. This work includes the pharmacological characterisation of the different
human subtypes and a gene expression analysis of the different subunits
PRINCIPAL INVESTIGATOR - BACKGROUND

Prof. Clementi holds a prominent position in several official scientific organisations in
Italy (pharmacology, toxicology, cell biology), as well as in internationat bodies
(Intemational Cell Biology Association). He is co-author of several books, author of
numerous publications, and is member of the editorial board of scientific periodicals.
