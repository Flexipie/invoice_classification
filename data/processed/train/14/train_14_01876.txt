I. RL

Extended pilot plant runs of RL containing 3 levels of DAP have been
completed. In another test, flavorants and DAP were run, All materials
were added in the sizing liquor. Also, a run was made where flavorants
were added to the pulp and DAP in the éize. The purpose of this work
ig to establish processing techniques for the addition of casings and
flavors to RL. Samples of the above materials are being evaluated
chemically and subjectively.

Marlboro-type cigarettes prepared at che factory containing cased
RL (Cased at R&D) are being evaluated. Preliminary screening by the
Flavor Group indicates the experimental cigarette to be smoother and
more flavorful than the untreated RL control, Smoke deliveries were
found to be similar (RL va. RCB).

Treatment of Schweitzer RL with DAP is currently being evaluated
for possible use in production cigarettes, Recently Schweitzer RL was
sprayed with DAP as would be required for production processing.
Cigarettes have been prepared and are being evaluated.

MW. ES

RP® Test #3544 has been completed. In this test ES cased without
PG was compared with normal cased ES in a Marlboro-type cigarette.
Results showed no significant differences between the two cigarettes.

Studies continue on the DAP treated ES at various DAP levels.
Additional pilot plant runs have been made at two DAP levels. In these
xuns, factory conditions were simulated for adaptation into factory
Processing. The resulting materials are being evaluated chemically
and subjectively.
