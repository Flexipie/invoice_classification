— FIELD TEST ESTIMATION OF REDUCING
SUGARS AND NICOTINE ALKALOIDS
IN TOBACCO LEAF ®

W. R. Harvey and A, M, Palmer

PHILIP MORRIS RESEARCH CENTER

Richmond, Virginia U.S.A.

ABSTRACT

A simple field test estimation of reducing sugars
and nicotine alkaloids on the same carbon clarified acidic
extract of tobacco leaf is presented, Reducing sugars are
estimated by their reaction with 2,3,5-triphenyltetrazolium
chloride in basic medium. Nicotine alkaloids are determined
by the coupling reaction between nicotine, cyanogen bromide
and 4,4°-diaminostilbene-2,2'-disulfonic acid. Readout is
by simple colorimeter or by visual Nessier tube comparison
with standards, Comparison with AutoAnalyzer reducing sugars
and nicotine alkaloid values and those of the field test are
presented.
