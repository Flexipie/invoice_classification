‘Summary: This research program evalustes the potential motivational impact of nicotine in various
animal models and directly compares the effects of nicotine with effects tom prototypical
addictive drugs. Some studies also examine the effects of other nonaddictive substances
that have mild stimulatory properties. A systematic evaluation of nicatine's motivational
effects should help place nicotine use in its proper position with other substances that are
commonly used by humans.
