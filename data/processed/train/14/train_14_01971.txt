1) In the context of Project EEC, sixty-six (66) samples of
tobacco from various origins (FR, IT, GR, DB, BE, PO) were
sent to Onnena for TLA analyses. The scope of this project is
to collect analytical data on the European tobacco types/
qualities which are not bought by PME and maybe find some
Grades which might be suitable for PME blends.
